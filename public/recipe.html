RECIPE:

How to trick your husband into taking you to a Taylor Swift concert 😈

Let’s be real: some of you have husbands who vibe with your pop music, bake you cookies, and join your TikTok dances. If that's the case—this recipe isn’t for you. For the rest of us, it’s time to get creative.

Ingredients

  • A banger of a playlist with all of her best "rock on" songs

  • THE outfit - preferably something with a “wow” factor. Sparkles help.

  • Three or four inspirational videos of husbands surprising their wives with concert tickets

  • An “urgent” email about the concert tickets going on sale soon (because urgency makes him act fast)

Instructions

  1. Begin playing her music regularly at home, in the car, or even when he’s working. Let him accidentally hear it often.

  2. Start wearing the outfit, like weekly. Think of it as a “look at how adorable I am, and how much you want to make me happy” type of ensemble. Make him start wondering when/where you could wear it out. Plant the seed, set the tone. Did I mention that sparkles help?

  3. Carefully schedule those videos. Send him one right after you made him breakfast, after you did him a favor, etc. Timing is key.

  4. We both know you're already subscribed to the Swiftie inbox list. Get ready to forward him that flashy email when the tickets go on sale. Catch him on a real good day.. like those biweekly Fridays or something, youknowwhatimean?👀
